    AstroSynchronous is a chaotic, bullet-hell style shoot 'em up with a twist: To deal damage, you must store and discharge energy by allowing your ship to get hit by bullets! Using this unique ability, you must destroy the threat that seeks to devour the Sun‚Äîthe Cosmic Jellyfish‚Äîby absorbing and weaponizing the blasts of energy it fires at you.

Over approximately one month, we followed a scrum workflow across 4 sprints to create a game built around the design verbs "connect" and "rush".
